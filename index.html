About me

CIARA BROWN

SOFTWARE DEVELOPER

We all have a moment in our life where we get an ah-ha moment, and that moment for me was in 2020. At that point I had been in the medical laboratory field for 10+ years and I began to have a strong desire to dive into a new field, specifically a field in tech. Growing up my family had a computer in our household, that my siblings and I used mostly for video games, and it wasn't until the Myspace days that I began to recognize code and the importance that it held. Fast-forward and throughout my career, technology was still a part of my life, but from a user standpoint. I started to become curious about the backend of technology so I decided to take a leap of faith, quit my comfortable job at a big pharmaceutical company here in Indianapolis, and dove right into learning software development with Eleven Fifty Academy. I am truly excited about this career feild and I have a feeling that this is going to be an interesting journey.
